HOUSE NO. 1

House No. 1 is the first house concept in a series of design experiments which investigate ideas surrounding notions of a “vertical ground” for architecture. Conceived as a recombination of familiar formal archetypes- the barn house, the cubic modular unit, a post modern amoeba shell- it is a case study for an “architecture upon architecture”.

'

This house design features 3-4 bedrooms with 3 bathrooms. Occupying roughly 3200 sq. ft, this precast concrete structure includes open living, and kitchen design, as well as a multi-purpose mezzanine level. Inspired by Kazuo Shinohara’s “savage machines”, the house design also features a modern take on the Japanese “tatami” room.
