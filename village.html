2022 HANGZHOU ASIAN OLYMPICS: ATHLETIC VILLAGE

This winning competition entry for the 2018 Hangzhou Olympic Village Competition features three cultural institutions: a performing arts center, a library, and a museum.

Expected to be built for the upcoming Hangzhou 2022 Asian Olympic Games, the proposal synthesizes traditional Chinese design sensibilities with contemporary architectural forms.
